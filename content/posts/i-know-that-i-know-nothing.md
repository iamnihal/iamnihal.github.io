We unknowingly reject things due to the lack of our understanding of the broader reality they exist within. We must acknowledge the fact that we don't even know the *knowns*, let alone the *unknowns*.

It makes sense why [Socrates](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Socrates) said:
> **"I know that I know nothing."**
